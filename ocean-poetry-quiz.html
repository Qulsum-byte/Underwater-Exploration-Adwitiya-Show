Ocean Poetry

Tides of Wonder

"As I look from the edge of the ocean, Glimmering golden, for it's almost dawn, So many waves coming towards me, And yet I know, there's a lot more going on. The ocean stretches for miles around, So many creatures with fins and gills, So many miles below the ground. The waves are splashing closer and closer, As I realise the world is bigger than it seems, As I stare into the ocean, In golden light, it gleams."

Sunlit waves

"The ocean's waves dance, back and forth they sway, Under the bright blue sky, on this sun-kissed day. Warm air caresses my skin, with a gentle, soothing breeze, As the sweet scent of saltwater fills the air, like a delicate tease. The water's deep blue hue, with hints of white, invites, As waves roll in and out, in a mesmerizing, rhythmic delight. Sunbeams kiss the surface, and diamonds start to shine, Glistening against the light, like a masterpiece, divine."

Sunlit Horizons

"As morning breaks, the sky's ablaze, With hues of gold, and shades of praise. The sun rises high, its radiance bright, Illuminating all, in its warm, golden light. The waves awaken, with a gentle roar, As the tide rises, to the sun's sweet score. The ocean's vastness, stretches far and wide, A canvas of wonder, where sunbeams reside. The breeze whispers secrets, as the palms sway, In rhythm with the waves, on this sun-kissed day. The world's alive, with vibrant color and sound, As sunlit horizons, stretch all around."
